Robotic Journey

My experience with robotics began at the age of 10, during a science fair where I built a functional wind power

plant. Since then, I have been developing projects independently, learning C++ for Arduino, creating technical drawings, collaborating in a SENAI team project, and experimenting with practical applications in mechanical and electronic systems. More than the prototypes themselves, what defines my path is the persistence to learn on my own, the creativity to turn ideas into concrete projects, and the commitment to constantly evolve. For me, robotics is more than a technical field: it is proof that curiosity and dedication can be transformed into solid, applicable knowledge. [See Robotic projects]
